The journey so far

Real Progress. No Filter.

Updated weekly. Nothing is edited for good optics. What happened, happened.

May 2026 — Week 1

Baseline Established

Reviewed blood reports from Tata 1mg and GVK Diagnostics. Documented all current markers — sugar, iron, Vitamin D, and BP. Built a simple daily tracking sheet. Starting from zero.

May 2026 — Week 2

First Rules Locked In

Morning walk locked at 25 minutes minimum, every day without exception. All packaged food and added sugar removed from the house. Meal timings fixed for all three meals.

May 2026 — Week 3

Systems Added

Medication alarm set — never missed again. Water intake tracked daily. Bedtime made consistent. Direct link noticed between sleep and morning energy.

May 2026 — Week 4

First Signals

Noticeably better morning alertness. Walks extended to 35 minutes naturally. Plate discipline held strong all week. Doctor follow-up booked for June.
