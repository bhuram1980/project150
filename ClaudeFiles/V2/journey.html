May 2026
W1
Baseline Set

Both blood reports reviewed and documented in full. A shared tracking sheet created — weight, fasting sugar, water intake, steps, and sleep hours. This is week zero: knowing exactly where we start, with no adjustments yet. The goal this week is only to see the numbers clearly, without denial.

May 2026
W2
First Rules Locked In

Three rules introduced and made non-negotiable: morning walks every day without exception, all sugary drinks removed from the house, meal timing fixed — no eating after 8 PM. Father completed 6 out of 7 walks. Mother had two days where evening meals slipped past 8:30 — noted, not judged.

May 2026
W3
Systems Built

Medication alarm set on both phones. Water intake tracked — consistently at 6 glasses, working toward 8. Sleep consistency still irregular; both going to bed at different times each night. The gym installation also began this week — equipment arrived on a pallet jack. Workers setting up machines on the new grey tile floor.

May 2026
W4
First Signals

Both parents report better alertness in the mornings — something neither mentioned being an issue before, which means it had been normalised for years. Father's walks now at 35 minutes consistently. Doctor review booked for end of June. The gym sign went up at the entrance gate. My father walked in on day three, sat on the bench, and didn't lift anything. He just sat there looking at it. That was enough.
