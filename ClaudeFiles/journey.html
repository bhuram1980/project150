Baseline Set

Both blood reports reviewed and documented in full. A shared tracking sheet created — weight, fasting sugar, water intake, steps, and sleep hours. This is week zero: knowing exactly where we start, with no adjustments yet. The goal this week is only to see the numbers clearly, without denial.

First Rules Locked In

Three rules introduced and made non-negotiable: morning walks every day without exception, all sugary drinks removed from the house, and meal timing fixed — no eating after 8 PM. Father completed 6 out of 7 walks this week. Mother had two days where evening meals slipped past 8:30 — noted, not judged.

Medication alarm set on both phones with a shared family reminder. Water intake now tracked — both parents consistently at 6 glasses, working toward 8. My father walked in on day three, sat on the bench, and didn't lift anything. He just sat there looking at it. That was enough.
