Learnings

What This Journey Is Teaching Us

Not expert opinions. Lived lessons — shared plainly so other families can start sooner.

What works

Consistency beats intensity.

25 minutes walked every day beats 2 hours once a week. Every time.

Family accountability works.

When everyone changes together, no one has to resist alone.

Simple food is sustainable food.

Portion control and timing matter more than exotic diets.

Walking is medicine.

More daily impact than any single dietary change we've made.

Still improving

Sleep timing is inconsistent.

We know the target. Hitting it every night is harder than expected.

Social events break discipline.

Weddings, festivals, and family visits still disrupt routine.

Report anxiety is real.

We are learning to separate effort from outcome.
