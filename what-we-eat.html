What We Eat

Simple food,
strict consistency.

Traditional Telugu home food. Measured portions. Repeatable routines.

Daily rhythm

Morning

Warm water + light protein breakfast.

Lunch

Main meal: vegetables, dal, controlled grains.

Evening

Fruit, nuts, or buttermilk only.

Dinner

Light plate, early timing, no heavy starch.

House rules

  • No sugary drinks at home.
  • No packaged snacks in routine days.
  • Fixed meal times are non-negotiable.
  • Fiber + protein with every major meal.
  • Hydration tracked daily.

High-starch items that can spike sugar quickly. We keep this visible in our kitchen as a daily reminder.
