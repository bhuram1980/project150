Our story

From Blood Reports to 150

The true origin of a family mission that started with a set of numbers we refused to accept.

In May 2026, I sat down with my father's and mother's blood reports from Tata 1mg and GVK Diagnostics. My father is 74. My mother is 66.

High blood sugar. Low iron. Low Vitamin D. Heart risk indicators. These were not abstract medical codes. These were warning signs about people I love.

We had two paths. Accept this as inevitable aging and manage decline, or build a real daily system and fight back together.

We chose to fight. Strict food discipline. Morning walks every day. Medicines on time. Better sleep. Honest tracking.

"That is Project 150. A family health journal. Nothing more, nothing less."

No coaching No products No memberships Just the truth

If our journey helps one more family begin theirs, this mission is worth it.
