Our Story

From Blood
Reports to 150

The true origin of a family mission that started with a set of numbers we refused to accept.

In May 2026, I sat down with my father's and mother's blood reports — from Tata 1mg and GVK Diagnostics. My father is 74. My mother is 66.

🔴  High blood sugar  ·  🟡  Low iron  ·  🟡  Low Vitamin D  ·  🔴  Heart risk indicators

These were not abstract medical codes. These were warning signs about people I love. The kind of numbers that could quietly take years away from their life if ignored.

We had two paths. Accept it as inevitable aging — manage the decline, wait for the next bad report. Or build a real, daily system and fight back together.

We chose to fight. Strict food discipline. Morning walks every single day. Medicines taken on time. Sleep made consistent. And we chose to document all of it — honestly, publicly — so other families could see what the real path looks like, not just the highlight reel.

"That is Project 150.
A family health journal.
Nothing more, nothing less."
