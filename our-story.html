Our Story

From blood reports to 150

The true origin of a family mission that started with a set of numbers we refused to ignore.

The turning point

In May 2026, I reviewed my father’s (74) and mother’s (66) reports from Tata 1mg and GVK Diagnostics. The numbers were clear: high sugar, low iron, low Vitamin D, and elevated heart risk indicators.

Those reports became a family decision point. We could accept it as “normal aging” or we could fight back with a disciplined system.

What we chose

Strict meals. Daily walking. Medicines on time. Better sleep. Honest tracking. No excuses. No shortcuts.

Project 150 is the public journal of that choice. We share what works, what fails, and what changes each week.

“That is Project 150. A family health journal. Nothing more, nothing less.”
